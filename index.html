My grandmother's walnut potica, passed down three generations. The secret is toasting the walnuts and adding a splash of rum to the filling. Here's the core of what makes it sing:

🌰 Walnut Filling (the heart of it)

  • 500g finely ground walnuts
  • 150g warm honey
  • 2 tbsp dark rum
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 2 egg whites, whisked stiff
  • Pinch of cinnamon, pinch of clove
