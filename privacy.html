2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide

  • Account registration: If you create an account via email/password or Google OAuth, we receive your email address, display name, and profile photo (if provided by Google).

  • Usage data: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous data about how you use the Site, including pages visited, time on site, and device type. GA4 uses cookies.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites.

2c. Information from Third Parties

If you sign in with Google, we receive from Google the information you authorize, typically: your name, email address, and profile picture. We do not receive your Google password.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Site and community features

  • To understand aggregate site usage via Google Analytics

4. Cookies

We use cookies for two purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Set by Supabase to maintain your login session. These are required for the Site to function and do not require consent.
  • Advertising and analytics cookies: Set by Google (AdSense and GA4). These are only activated after you click "Accept" on our consent banner. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

You can opt out of Google's use of cookies for advertising at any time by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database platform with servers located in the United States. Supabase employs industry-standard security measures. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access and modify data they are authorized to access.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
